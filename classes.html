Zen Yoga combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to promote relaxation, flexibility, and mental clarity. The class focuses on mindful movement and inner awareness, making it ideal for stress relief and improving overall well-being. Participants engage in a series of poses held for varying durations, enhancing both physical and mental balance.

  • Improves flexibility and balance
  • Reduces Stress anxiety
  • Enhances mental focus and clarity

Zen Pilates emphasizes core strength, flexibility, and overall body conditioning. The class involves controlled movements targeting the core, lower back, hips, and thighs. It aims to improve posture, balance, and coordination through precise and mindful exercises, often incorporating small equipment like bands or balls.

  • Strengthens core muscles
  • Improves posture and alignment
  • Enhances overall body conditioning

Zen HIIT is a dynamic class designed to provide a full-body workout through short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief rest periods.   • Boosts cardiovascular health
  • Burns calories quickly
  • Builds muscle strength and endurance
